# Warranty Information

## Standard Warranty
All RetailCo electronics include a **12-month manufacturer warranty** from date of delivery.

## Extended Warranty
- Available at checkout for electronics over $200
- Extends coverage to **3 years**
- Covers mechanical/electrical failure only (not accidental damage)

## What's Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Dead on arrival (DOA) — contact within 7 days
- Battery failure within first 6 months

## What's NOT Covered
- Accidental damage, drops, liquid damage
- Normal wear and tear
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Commercial use (unless product rated for commercial)

## Warranty Claims Process
1. Contact support with order ID and description of issue
2. Support may request photos or video
3. If approved: repair, replace, or refund at RetailCo discretion
4. Turnaround: **7–14 business days** for assessment

## Homewares
Non-electronic homewares: **6-month** warranty against manufacturing defects only.

## Contact
Warranty claims: warranty@retailco.com.au or support portal with order ID.
