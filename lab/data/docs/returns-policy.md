# Returns & Refunds Policy

## Return Window
- **30 days** from delivery date for most items
- Extended to **60 days** during December (holiday purchases)

## Eligible Items
- Unused items in original packaging
- Items with manufacturing defects (any time within warranty period)

## Non-Returnable Items
- Opened personal care products (pillows, mattress toppers) unless defective
- Clearance/final sale items (marked at purchase)
- Gift cards

## Opened Items
Opened items may be returned within 30 days if:
- Item is defective, OR
- Wrong item was shipped by RetailCo

A **15% restocking fee** applies to opened non-defective items.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within **5–7 business days** after item is received at warehouse
- Refund method matches original payment method
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless RetailCo error

## How to Return
1. Log into retailco.com.au/account
2. Select order → "Return item"
3. Print prepaid return label (free for defective/wrong items)
4. Drop at any Australia Post location

## Exchanges
Exchanges available for different size/color of same product. Contact support if product is discontinued.
