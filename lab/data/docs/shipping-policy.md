# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: **5–7 business days** for metro areas
- Delivery time: **7–10 business days** for regional areas
- Cost: Free for orders over $75; otherwise $9.95 flat rate

## Express Shipping
- Delivery time: **1–2 business days** (metro only)
- Cost: $14.95 flat rate

## Tracking
All orders receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours of dispatch. Track at retailco.com.au/track using your order ID.

## International
RetailCo currently ships within Australia only. International shipping is not available.
