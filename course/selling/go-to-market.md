# Go-To-Market — Selling the FDE Bootcamp Online

A practical launch playbook to start selling the course.

---

## 1. Positioning

**One-liner:**
> "Become a Forward Deployed Engineer — build and deploy a real AI pilot, and land $180k–$350k+ customer-facing roles."

**Unique angle (your moat):**
- Taught by a 14-year platform/SRE + AI Ops engineer (credibility)
- Students ship a *real deployable project*, not toy notebooks
- Covers the rare combo: customer skills + AI building + production deployment

**Target buyer personas:**
1. Mid-level backend/SRE/DevOps engineers wanting to pivot
2. Solutions engineers/SAs wanting hands-on AI building
3. Career-switchers with fundamentals who need a differentiator

---

## 2. Pricing & Packaging

| Tier | Price (USD) | Includes | Goal |
|------|-------------|----------|------|
| **Self-paced** | $149–$299 | Videos, slides, lab repo, quizzes, capstone | Volume |
| **Pro** | $399–$599 | + Interview prep module, AWS deep-dive, certificate | Margin |
| **Cohort** | $799–$1,499 | + Live sessions, mock interviews, feedback, community | Premium |
| **Corporate** | $5k+/team | Team workshop, custom customer scenario | B2B |

**Launch tactic:** Founding-cohort discount (40–50% off) for first 20–50 students in exchange for testimonials.

**Payment:** Stripe / platform checkout. Offer a 14-day refund to lower buying friction.

---

## 3. Platforms — Where to Host & Sell

| Platform | Best for | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Teachable / Podia / Thinkific** | Own-brand self-paced | You keep margin + email list |
| **Gumroad** | Fast launch, digital download | Low friction, good for v1 |
| **Udemy** | Reach/discovery | Big audience, low price, high cut — use for funnel |
| **Maven** | Cohort-based premium | Great for $800+ live cohorts |
| **YouTube (free)** | Top-of-funnel marketing | Free mini-lessons → paid course |

**Recommended combo:** Free YouTube clips (marketing) → own platform (Teachable/Podia) for the paid course → Maven for premium cohorts.

---

## 6. Launch Sequence (8 weeks)

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1–2 | Record Chapters 1–2; set up landing page + email capture |
| 3 | Publish free Chapter 1 on YouTube; start LinkedIn build-in-public |
| 4 | Release lead magnet (FDE Skills Checklist); grow email list |
| 5 | Record Chapters 3–5; open founding-cohort waitlist |
| 6 | Pre-sell to waitlist at founding discount |
| 7 | Official launch; daily social proof + demos |
| 8 | Collect testimonials; raise price; plan first live cohort |

---

## 7. Landing Page Structure

1. **Hero:** one-liner + "Build a real AI pilot in 5 chapters" + CTA
2. **Problem:** "You're a great engineer but locked out of customer-facing AI roles"
3. **Outcome:** what they'll build (RetailCo pilot screenshot/demo video)
4. **Curriculum:** the 5 chapters (from `README.md`)
5. **Instructor bio:** your 14-year credibility + AI Ops work
6. **Capstone project:** the deployable portfolio piece
7. **Pricing + guarantee:** tiers + 14-day refund
8. **Testimonials:** (collect from founding cohort)
9. **FAQ:** prerequisites, time commitment, refunds
10. **Final CTA**

---

## 8. Social Proof Plan

- Founding cohort at a discount → testimonials + capstone demos
- Encourage students to post their deployed pilot on LinkedIn (tag you)
- Track and publish outcomes: "Student X landed an FDE role"

---

## 9. Metrics to Track

| Metric | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Landing page conversion | Funnel health |
| Email list growth | Future launches |
| Free → paid conversion | Funnel efficiency |
| Completion rate | Course quality (drives refunds/referrals) |
| Refund rate | Content-promise fit |
| Student outcomes | Best marketing asset |
